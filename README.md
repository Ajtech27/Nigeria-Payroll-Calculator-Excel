 # PayTrack Pro — Nigerian Employee Payroll Calculator 🇳🇬

A fully automated monthly payroll system built in Microsoft Excel, specifically designed for Nigerian businesses. Enter your staff details once and update three things every month — the entire payroll calculates itself.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

PayTrack Pro eliminates the manual effort of monthly payroll processing for small to mid-sized Nigerian businesses. It handles every statutory deduction automatically — PAYE tax across all 6 income bands, pension contributions, NHF, and NHIS — and generates a professional payslip for each employee with a single input.

Built for businesses with 5 to 50 staff who are currently processing payroll manually or using a basic spreadsheet with no automation.

---

## 📂 What's Inside — 6 Sheets

| Sheet | Description |
|---|---|
| ⚙️ **Settings** | Company name, pay period, work hours, overtime multiplier, and all statutory rates. Change rates once here and everything updates everywhere |
| 👥 **Employees** | Register of employees with basic salary, transport, housing, meal, and tax-exempt allowances. Gross salary auto-calculates |
| 🕐 **Timesheet** | Monthly attendance tracking per employee. Enter days present, days absent, and overtime hours — regular pay and overtime calculate automatically |
| 💰 **Payroll** | The engine. Calculates pension, NHF, NHIS, taxable income, PAYE tax using Nigeria's full 6-band formula, total deductions, and final net pay |
| 📄 **Payslip** | Enter any employee row number and a clean, printable payslip generates instantly showing all earnings, deductions, attendance summary, and net pay |
| 📊 **Dashboard** | Total headcount, gross payroll, net payroll, PAYE tax, pension, and overtime at a glance — plus a department-by-department breakdown |

## ✅ Features

- **Automated PAYE Calculation** — Full Nigeria 6-band tax structure applied correctly to every employee based on their taxable income
- **Statutory Deductions** — Pension (8%), NHF (2.5%), and NHIS (5%) calculated automatically for every staff member
- **Overtime Tracking** — Enter overtime hours and the system calculates the additional pay at the configured multiplier
- **One-Click Payslip Generator** — Enter an employee number and a professional, print-ready payslip appears instantly
- **Live Dashboard** — All key payroll figures update automatically as staff data changes
- **Colour-Coded Inputs** — Blue cells are editable inputs. Black cells are calculated formulas. Anyone on your team can use it without training
- **Scalable Settings** — All statutory rates are stored in one Settings sheet. Update once and the entire file updates everywhere

## 🚀 How to Use It

**Step 1 — Settings Sheet**
- Enter your company name and pay period
- Confirm or update the statutory rates — PAYE bands, pension, NHF, NHIS, and overtime multiplier

**Step 2 — Employees Sheet**
- Enter each staff member's details — name, department, basic salary, and allowances
- Gross salary calculates automatically

**Step 3 — Timesheet Sheet**
- At the end of each month, enter days present, days absent, and overtime hours for each employee
- Regular pay and overtime pay calculate automatically

**Step 4 — Payroll Sheet**
- All deductions and net pay calculate automatically from the timesheet and employee data
- No manual input needed on this sheet

**Step 5 — Payslip Sheet**
- Type the employee's row number into the input cell
- A complete, professional payslip generates instantly
- Print or export as PDF to share with the employee

**Step 6 — Dashboard Sheet**
- Review the monthly payroll summary
- All figures update live as you update the timesheet

## 📋 Statutory Rates Applied

|---|---|
| Pension (Employee) | 8% of gross salary |
| NHF | 2.5% of basic salary |
| NHIS | 5% of basic salary |
| PAYE | 6-band progressive tax on taxable income |
